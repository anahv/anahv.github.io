my portfolio

LEVEL
LEVEL is a new low-cost long-haul airline. I was product owner and lead designer of their new website, flylevel.com.
Zenda
Zenda is an e-commerce start-up within my company. I created the name, logo and visual identity for the business, and remain Zenda's creative lead. You can check it out in zendaglobal.com.
playerbakes
One of my favourite side projects - I created the brand identity for a sourdough microbakery. This included creating the logo, colours and brand guidelines. Have a look at playerbakes.com.
