Zenda is a start-up inside of IAG, the owner of British Airways. It uses the space in BA airplanes to ship parcels door to door.

I am currently Managing Director for Zenda, reporting to the IAG Cargo CEO. When I first joined in 2018 as Head of Product, I launched the first version of its API. I then went on to design and product manage a UI dashboard, and several eCommerce plugins. 18 months after starting, I became MD, where I continue to look after the product plus manage all commercial and business development activity and financial forecasts.

Zenda also has a special place in my heart as I came up with its name (it's based on the Spanish word "senda", which means "path" or "track") and brand, saving thousands of pounds by outpitching a creative agency. It was my first brand design and was a stepping stone to creating several brands after that success.

  • Category: Product Management, Brand Design, UI, UX Design

In my spare time, I learned C (a rather harsh introduction to programming!), Python, SQL and JavaScript with Harvard University's CS50 Introduction to Computer Science, an online course in edX. The course was a great introduction to computer science and programming, and included concepts like abstraction, algorithms, data structures and software engineering.

Nugget was my final project, an iOS app that lets you record and then reminds you of your best ideas. You can view the code in Github.

Stretching Timer

I wanted a countdown timer that pinged every time I had to change into the next stretch, but the ones I found online were ugly, had unpleasant sound effects, or were full of ads. So I applied what I learned in the Complete 2020 Web Development Bootcamp and built one with React.js. This is only the beginning of what I plan to build!

You can give Stretching Timer a go in Heroku or view the code in Github.

  • Tech stack: React.js, Express.js, Node.js, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
  • Category: Graphic Design
